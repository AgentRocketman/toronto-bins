## Toronto Open Data — Night Collection Research
- City of Toronto open data has NO day/night collection flag
- Only 8 calendar zones: Tue1/2, Wed1/2, Thu1/2, Fri1/2 (no Monday)
- Night collection zones are street-boundary based, NOT in the open data API
- 4 overnight corridors confirmed from City website:
  - Monday night: East of Bathurst, south of Bloor/Danforth to Victoria Park
  - Tuesday night: Both sides of Bloor E&W between Bathurst and Danforth
  - Wednesday night: Both sides of Bathurst north of Bloor W
  - Thursday night: Both sides of Bathurst south of Bloor W + Bloor W west of Bathurst
- Detection method: lat/lng coordinate polygon check (not postal code)
- Businesses in night zones: apartments above commercial = yes (City collection), pure commercial = no (private contractors)

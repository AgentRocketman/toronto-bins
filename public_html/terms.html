1. About GetMyBin

GetMyBin is a residential bin management service operating in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. We roll your City of Toronto bins to the curb before your scheduled collection day and return them to your property afterward — so you never have to think about bin day again.

2. Service Area

GetMyBin serves residential addresses within the City of Toronto that have active City of Toronto curbside waste collection. Our service is not available at:

  • Condominiums and apartment buildings with private waste collection contracts
  • Commercial properties
  • Addresses outside the City of Toronto municipal boundary
  • Properties without accessible curbside collection

You can verify eligibility by entering your address at agentrocketman.com. We use the City of Toronto's open data to determine your collection schedule.

3. Services Offered

We offer two service types, which may be booked independently or together:

  • Roll Out — We place your bins at the curb the evening before your scheduled collection day. For addresses in overnight collection zones, Roll Out takes place early evening on the collection day itself.
  • Roll In — We return your bins from the curb to your property on the afternoon of your collection day. Roll In is not available for overnight collection areas.
  • Both — Roll Out and Roll In on the same collection day, charged as two separate service events. Not available for overnight collection areas.

We handle all bins that the City of Toronto collects at your address, including garbage, recycling, green bin (organics), and yard waste.

4. Booking & Scheduling

Services are booked through our website. The following conditions apply:

  • Ad Hoc bookings — You select specific upcoming collection dates. Payment is charged in full at the time of booking.
  • Recurring bookings — Your bins are serviced on every applicable collection day, automatically. You are billed weekly via a Stripe subscription.
  • Order cutoff — New orders for a given collection date must be placed by 4:00 PM Toronto time the day before collection (day collection areas) or by 4:00 PM Toronto time on the collection day (overnight collection areas). Orders placed after the cutoff will be scheduled for the next available collection date.
  • Holiday schedule changes — We follow the City of Toronto's adjusted collection schedule for statutory holidays. No action is required from you.

5. Pricing & Payment

All prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD) and exclude applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.

  • Ad Hoc rate: $8.95 per service event
  • Recurring rate: $5.95 per service event per week
  • Roll Out and Roll In are each a separate service event. Booking "Both" on the same day counts as two events.
  • Ontario HST (13%) is added at checkout and displayed before payment.

Payments are processed securely via Stripe. GetMyBin does not store your payment card details. For recurring customers, Stripe automatically charges your payment method weekly.

6. Cancellation Policy

48-hour rule: Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before your next scheduled collection. If a cancellation request is received within 48 hours of your next collection, that collection will proceed and the applicable charge applies.
  • You may cancel your service at any time via agentrocketman.com/manage.
  • For recurring subscriptions, cancellation stops all future billing immediately. Service continues through any collection within the 48-hour window.
  • For ad hoc bookings, dates beyond the 48-hour window at the time of cancellation will be automatically refunded to your original payment method. Dates within the 48-hour window are non-refundable as the service will be completed.
  • Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days depending on your financial institution.

7. Service Guarantee

If we miss your scheduled collection, you don't pay. If GetMyBin fails to complete a booked service event for any reason within our control, that event will be fully refunded or credited. No questions asked.

This guarantee does not apply in cases where access to the property is restricted, bins are locked or inaccessible, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control prevent service delivery (including severe weather, road closures, or City of Toronto collection cancellations).

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GetMyBin ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website at agentrocketman.com or book our bin collection services. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Ontario privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver your service and communicate with you. Here's what we collect and why:

Information Why we collect it
Name To identify your booking and address you in communications
Email address To send booking confirmations, service updates, and cancellation notices
Phone number Optional — for service-related contact only
Service address To locate your property and schedule bin collection
Payment information To process payment for your booking. We do not store card details — all payment data is handled by Stripe (see Section 4)
Booking history To manage your account, process refunds, and resolve disputes

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government ID, social insurance numbers, or health data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • Delivering your booked bin collection service
  • Sending booking confirmations, service reminders, and updates
  • Processing payments and issuing refunds where applicable
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notifying you of service changes, schedule updates, or cancellations
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not send unsolicited promotional emails.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the basis of:

  • Contract performance — to deliver the service you booked
  • Legitimate interests — to manage our business operations, prevent fraud, and improve our service
  • Legal obligation — where required by Canadian law

4. Third Parties We Share Data With

We share your information only with the following trusted third parties, strictly as needed to deliver your service:

Third Party Purpose Privacy Policy
Stripe Payment processing. Stripe handles all card data securely. We never see or store your full card details. stripe.com/privacy
Airtable Booking and order management. Your booking details are stored in our Airtable database. airtable.com/privacy
City of Toronto Open Data We use the City of Toronto's publicly available collection schedule data to look up your bin day. No personal information is shared with the City. toronto.ca/open-data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Active bookings — retained for the duration of your service
  • Completed or cancelled bookings — retained for up to 2 years for accounting, dispute resolution, and legal compliance
  • Payment records — retained as required by Canadian tax law (minimum 6 years)

After the applicable retention period, your information is securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. These include:

  • HTTPS encryption on all web pages and API communications
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant
  • Restricted access to booking data on a need-to-know basis

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that collect personal data. The only third-party scripts loaded on our site are:

  • Stripe.js — required to securely process payments
  • City of Toronto geocoder — required to look up your address

8. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate information be corrected
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent to our use of your information (subject to legal and contractual restrictions)
  • Complaint — file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@agentrocketman.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
